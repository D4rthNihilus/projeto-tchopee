Who we are?

Tchopee's headquarters in Brazil

Tchopee is a Brazilian multinational company dedicated to the trade of toys and a wide variety of objects used by the characters of Tom's Crew.

It was founded in 2014 by entrepreneur Octavius Drupen in partnership with the show's production company, with the purpose of serving fans so they can feel like part of this magical and fun universe. Our target audience is especially children, and for this reason, we prioritize quality and safety in our products, strictly following all the standards established by Inmetro.
